### **SATURDAY: LONG RUN**

**🔥 KEY WORKOUT - Progressive Long Run 🔥**

**Weekly Progression:**

**Weeks 21-23:** 18-19km
- First 13km @ 5:45-6:00/km (easy, conversational)
- Last 5-6km @ 5:00-5:15/km (race pace or slightly faster)
- Total time: ~1:45-2:00

**Weeks 24-26:** 20-21km
- First 15km easy (5:45-6:00/km)
- Last 6km @ 4:50-5:05/km (pushing pace)
- Total time: ~1:50-2:05

**Weeks 27-29:** 22km (LONGER THAN RACE DISTANCE!)
- First 16km easy (5:45-6:00/km)
- Last 6km @ 5:15-5:30/km (controlled fast finish)
- Total time: ~2:00-2:10
- **Mental win: "If I can do 22km with fast finish, 21km race will feel easier"**

**Weeks 30-32:** Taper for August race
- Week 30: 20km (16 easy + 4 @ race pace)
- Week 31: 18km (14 easy + 4 @ race pace)
- Week 32: Race week - 12km easy OR race day

**Weeks 33-36:** Back to building
- Week 33: 16km (recovery from race)
- Week 34: 20km (15 easy + 5 @ pace)
- Week 35: Race week - 12km easy OR race day
- Week 36: Race week - 10km easy OR race day

**Pre-Long Run Protocol:**
- Wake 2 hours before run
- Light breakfast 90min before:
  - 2 slices toast with honey + banana, OR
  - Oats (60g) with banana + honey
  - Coffee (if normal routine)
  - 500ml water
- Bathroom
- 5min dynamic warm-up
- Start easy (first km should feel too slow)

**During Run (for 20km+):**
- Carry small water bottle OR plan route with water fountains
- Small sips every 15-20min (don't chug)
- If going 22km: Consider one energy gel at 60min mark (practice for race)

**Post-Long Run Protocol:**
- 5min walk immediately (don't stop suddenly)
- Drink 500-750ml water
- Eat banana + protein source within 30min
- Stretch: 15min comprehensive
- Ice bath OR cold shower (10min) if available
- Elevate legs for 20min

**Afternoon:**
- Foam rolling: 20-30min (thorough, all muscle groups)
- Deep stretching: 20min
- Nap: 30-60min if possible
- Refuel: Proper meal with protein + carbs

### **GYM WORK (PHASE 3):**

**Frequency:** 1x per week (maintenance only)

**When:** Monday evening (if feeling fresh) OR Thursday evening (if took rest)

**Session (30min max):**

1. **Nordic Curls: 2 sets x 8-10 reps**
   - Still critical
   - Never stop these
   - Injury prevention #1

2. **Trap Bar Deadlifts: 3 sets x 5 reps**
   - Moderate weight (not grinding)
   - Explosive lift, controlled lower
   - Maintain strength

3. **Core Circuit: 10min**
   - Planks: 3x30-45sec
   - Dead bugs: 3x12
   - Pallof press: 3x10 each side

**That's it.**

**Why minimal gym:**
- Running volume is HIGH (58-65km weeks)
- Recovery capacity is limited
- Risk of overtraining if adding too much
- Focus: Maintain strength, prevent injury
- Not: Build muscle, get stronger

**If legs are tired, skip gym entirely**
- Foam roll at home instead
- Stretching and mobility
- Sleep and nutrition are more important
