## **CONTENT SYSTEMS (Avoid Burnout):**

### **Filming:**
- **Morning runs:** Phone mount on chest OR handheld
- **Meal prep:** Tripod + time-lapse (set it and forget it)
- **Progress photos:** Same spot, same time, Sunday 6am (30 seconds)

### **Editing:**
- **CapCut** (free, mobile, perfect for Reels/TikTok)
- **InShot** (backup option)
- Create 3-4 go-to templates (saves time)
- Don't overedit - raw is often better

### **Batch Creation:**
- **Sunday:** Film weekly recap + next week preview (30min)
- **Meal prep day:** Film entire cooking process (set up phone, let it run)
- **Long run Saturday:** Film entire run (pull 5-10 clips for week's content)

### **Scheduling:**
- **Later** or **Meta Business Suite** (free scheduling)
- Schedule 3-5 posts Sunday night for the week
- Frees up mental space during busy week
- Still do daily stories (can't schedule those - keeps it fresh)

## **NON-NEGOTIABLES:**

**1. CONSISTENCY > PERFECTION**
- Posted every day beats perfect post once a week
- Algorithm rewards consistency
- Your audience wants regular updates

**2. AUTHENTICITY > POLISH**
- Shaky phone footage of real struggle > perfectly edited fake enthusiasm
- Your audience wants REAL
- Show the bad runs, the struggles, the doubts

**3. VALUE > VANITY METRICS**
- 1,000 engaged followers > 10,000 dead followers
- Reply to comments, build relationships
- Help others, don't just flex
- Community over clout

**4. LONG-TERM > SHORT-TERM**
- This is 9-month journey MINIMUM
- Building audience takes time
- Stay patient, stay consistent
- Trust the process (just like training)

**5. STORY > STATS**
- "I struggled through 6km today" > "6km at 6:15 pace"
- People connect with struggle, not statistics
- Show the process, not just results
- Be vulnerable, be human

## **THE FIRST 30 DAYS (Critical Period):**

**Your only job: POST EVERY SINGLE DAY for 30 days.**

Doesn't matter if it's "good" content. Just show up.

**Why:**
- Builds YOUR habit (content creation becomes automatic)
- Algorithm learns your content (starts showing it to more people)
- You learn what resonates (see what gets engagement)
- Builds momentum (consistency compounds)

**30-day challenge within the 280-day challenge:**
- Daily post (feed or TikTok minimum)
- 5+ stories daily
- Reply to every comment
- Engage with 20+ accounts in your niche

**By Day 30:**
- You'll have data on what works (which content gets views/engagement)
- You'll have developed content rhythm (it becomes natural)
- You'll have initial audience (even if small - they're loyal)
- You'll have momentum (algorithm favors you)

## **IF YOU GO VIRAL:**

**What to do when a post blows up (100k+ views):**

1. **Pin it to your profile** (new visitors see it first)

2. **Create follow-up content**
   - "Since this blew up, here's what happened next..."
   - Ride the wave while it lasts

3. **Engage with EVERY comment** (as much as possible)
   - Reply to top comments
   - Pin funny/insightful comments
   - Ask questions back
   - Build relationships

4. **Direct traffic to other platforms**
   - "For daily updates, follow my Instagram"
   - "Full weekly vlogs on YouTube"
   - Convert viral viewers to followers

5. **Don't change strategy**
   - Keep posting consistently
   - Don't try to force another viral post
   - Stay authentic to what worked
