### **THE TAPER PHILOSOPHY:**

**What is tapering:**
- Reducing training volume (run less distance)
- Maintaining training intensity (keep some speed work)
- Allowing body to fully recover and supercompensate
- Fresh legs + maintained fitness = peak performance

**Common taper mistakes to AVOID:**
- ❌ Panic training: "I haven't done enough, I need one more long run"
- ❌ Complete rest: "I'll just stop running entirely"
- ❌ Changing routine: "Let me try this new thing..."
- ❌ Overeating: "I need to carb load for 3 weeks"

**What TO do:**
- ✅ Trust the process (9 months of training is enough)
- ✅ Reduce volume ~40-50% from peak
- ✅ Keep short, sharp speed work
- ✅ Maintain normal routine until final week
- ✅ Focus on sleep, hydration, mental prep
- ✅ Visualize success daily
