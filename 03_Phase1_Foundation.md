# **3. PHASE 1: FOUNDATION BUILD**

## **"THE 75 TO RUNNER CHALLENGE"**
## **WEEKS 1-12 (FEBRUARY 15 - MAY 8, 2026)**

### **DURATION: 84 DAYS (12 weeks)**

**Why 84 days instead of 75?**
- Week 1: Days 1-7
- Week 2: Days 8-14
- Week 11: Days 71-77
- Week 12: Days 78-84
- We're calling Days 1-75 "The 75 To Runner Challenge"
- Days 76-84 are your first recovery week post-challenge

---

### **PRIMARY GOALS:**

1. ✅ Complete 75-day accountability challenge (Days 1-75)
2. ✅ Build aerobic base from scratch
3. ✅ Lose 5-7kg (95kg → 88-90kg)
4. ✅ Run 16km long run continuously
5. ✅ Strengthen weak hamstrings
6. ✅ Establish unbreakable discipline and daily habits

### **TRAINING PHILOSOPHY:**

- **Easy running builds your engine** - 80% of runs at conversational pace
- **Consistency beats intensity** - show up every single day
- **Recovery is training** - one mandatory rest day per week
- **Progressive overload** - small weekly increases, never big jumps
- **Listen to your body** - soreness is OK, pain is NOT

---

## **THE 75 TO RUNNER RULES:**

**Daily Non-Negotiables (Every Day for 75 Days - Feb 15 to May 1):**

1. ✅ **One workout minimum** (45min+)
   - Follow the training plan below
   - Quality over quantity
   
2. ✅ **1 gallon water** (3.8L)
   - Track throughout day
   - Use marked bottle or count fills
   
3. ✅ **No junk food / No takeaways**
   - All home-cooked meals
   - Your meal rotation plan (see Nutrition section)
   - No chips, chocolates, sweets, fast food
   
4. ✅ **Bible reading**
   - Genesis to Revelation in 75 days
   - ~4 chapters daily or audio equivalent
   - Can split AM/PM
   
5. ✅ **Daily progress documentation**
   - Morning: Progress photo (same spot, same pose)
   - Evening: Log workout in Strava + notes
   
6. ✅ **Track everything**
   - Complete daily checklist
   - Log how you felt (1-10 scale)

7. ✅ **ONE mandatory rest day per week (Sunday)**
   - Recovery walk counts as "workout"
   - NO running on Sundays during Phase 1
   - This is non-negotiable for injury prevention

**Miss ANY requirement:** Add 1 day to the 75
*(More forgiving than "restart from Day 1", but still holds you accountable)*

**Example:** If on Day 23 you forget your progress photo, tomorrow becomes Day 23 (again). You now need 76 days total to complete the challenge.

### **MONDAY:**

**Morning 6:00am (Outdoor):**

**Easy Run - Progressive Volume**
- Week 1: 3km @ 7:15-7:30/km
- Week 2: 3.5km @ 7:00-7:15/km
- Week 3: 4km @ 7:00-7:15/km
- Week 4: 4km @ 6:45-7:00/km

**How to run:**
- Conversational pace (you could talk in full sentences)
- Nose breathing test: Can you breathe through nose? You're at right pace
- Route: Hatfield streets, LC de Villiers area, vary routes
- If you can't hold pace: SLOW DOWN (no ego here)

**After run:**
- 5min walk cool-down
- Stretch: Hamstrings, quads, calves, hip flexors (30sec each)
- Drink 500ml water

**Evening 21:00 (Home - Optional but Recommended):**

**Hamstring Strength Circuit - 20-25min**

*This addresses your weak hamstrings - YOUR #1 injury prevention priority*

**Warm-up (5min):**
- Leg swings: 10 forward/back, 10 side/side each leg
- Bodyweight squats: x15
- Glute bridges: x15

**Main Circuit:**

1. **Nordic Curls** (eccentric hamstring curls) - THE CRITICAL EXERCISE
   - **How to:** Kneel on soft surface, anchor feet under couch/bed/have partner hold, slowly lower body forward controlling with hamstrings, use hands to catch yourself, push back up to start
   - **Progression:**
     - Week 1: 3 sets x 3-4 reps (5sec eccentric lower)
     - Week 2: 3 sets x 4-5 reps
     - Week 3: 3 sets x 5 reps
     - Week 4: 3 sets x 6 reps
   - Rest: 90sec between sets
   - **THIS IS YOUR INJURY INSURANCE - never skip**

2. **Single-Leg RDLs** (Romanian Deadlifts)
   - Bodyweight or hold water bottle/backpack for resistance
   - 3 sets x 8 reps per leg
   - Focus: Balance, feel hamstring stretch at bottom
   - Rest: 60sec

3. **Glute Bridges**
   - 3 sets x 15 reps
   - Hold top position 2 seconds, squeeze glutes hard
   - Rest: 60sec

4. **Plank Hold**
   - 3 sets x 30-45sec
   - Keep core tight, don't let hips sag
   - Rest: 60sec

5. **Dead Bugs**
   - 3 sets x 12 reps total (6 each side)
   - Opposite arm/leg extensions
   - Keep lower back flat on ground
   - Rest: 60sec

**Cool-down (5min):**
- Hip flexor stretch: 60sec each side
- Hamstring stretch: 60sec each side
- Quad stretch: 60sec each side
- Child's pose: 60sec

**Total Monday: 45-50min minimum** ✅

**Run Progression:**

- Week 3: 4km easy @ 6:45-7:00/km
- Week 4: 4km easy @ 6:30-6:45/km

**Then add STRIDES:**
- Week 1: 4 x 100m strides @ 70% effort
- Week 2: 4 x 100m strides @ 70% effort
- Week 3: 6 x 100m strides @ 75% effort
- Week 4: 6 x 100m strides @ 75% effort

**What are strides:**
- 100m controlled accelerations (NOT all-out sprints)
- First 20m: Gradually build speed
- Middle 60m: Hold at 70-75% of max sprint speed
- Last 20m: Decelerate smoothly
- Walk back to start (1-2min recovery)
- **Purpose:** Maintain your sprint mechanics and speed without hard interval stress

**Total: 45-50min** ✅

**Post-Run Protocol (immediately after):**
- Drink 500ml water

- Stretch: 10min focusing on all major muscle groups
  - Hamstrings: 60sec each
  - Quads: 60sec each
  - Calves: 60sec each

**Afternoon 16:00 (Home or Virgin Active - Optional):**

**Recovery Protocol - Choose one:**

**Option A: Comprehensive Foam Rolling + Stretch (45min)**
- 20min foam rolling:
  - Spend 3-5min on each major muscle group
  - Quads, hamstrings, calves, IT band, glutes, back
  - Pause on tender spots (10-30sec)
  - Breathe through discomfort
- 25min deep stretching:
  - Hold each stretch 60-90sec
  - Focus on hips, hamstrings, quads
  - Breathe deeply, relax into stretch
  - Don't force it

**Option B: Swimming (if Virgin Active has pool) (45min)**
- Easy laps, very slow
- Just moving in water
- Great for recovery, takes weight off legs

**Option C: Ice Bath Recovery (if available) (45min total)**
- 10min cold water immersion
  - Fill bath with cold water + ice
  - Sit in up to waist
  - Water temp: 10-15°C
  - Breathe through discomfort
  - Reduces inflammation
- Then: 20min foam rolling + 15min stretching
