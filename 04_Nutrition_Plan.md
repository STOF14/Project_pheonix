## **4-Week Meal Rotation:**

### **WEEK 1:**

**Breakfasts:**

- Mon/Wed/Fri: Oats with banana and honey (trying oats!)
- Tue/Thu: Scrambled eggs with toast
- Sat/Sun: French toast OR Cheese on toast

**Lunches:**

- Mon-Wed: Polony & cheese sandwiches
- Thu-Fri: Leftover chicken stir-fry wraps
- Sat-Sun: Leftover dinners

**Dinners:**

- Mon: Chicken stir-fry with rice & veg
- Tue: Spaghetti bolognese (beef mince, tomato)
- Wed: Chicken curry with rice
- Thu: Beef mince tacos (homemade seasoning)
- Fri: Pap & wors with chakalaka
- Sat: Chicken schnitzel with chips & salad
- Sun: Roast chicken thighs with roast veg

---

### **WEEK 2:**

**Breakfasts:**

- Rotation: Oats / Eggs on toast / French toast

**Lunches:**

- Cheese & tomato sandwiches
- Instant noodles (upgraded with egg & veg)
- Leftover curry in bread

**Dinners:**

- Mon: Chicken biryani (spiced rice, chicken)
- Tue: Beef stroganoff with rice
- Wed: Crumbed hake with sweet potato chips
- Thu: Chicken sosaties with yellow rice
- Fri: Bunny chow (curry in hollowed bread)
- Sat: Pizza toast (tomato paste, cheese, polony)
- Sun: Chicken stew with dumplings

---

### **WEEK 3:**

**Breakfasts:**

- Rotation continues

**Lunches:**

- Toasted cheese & tomato
- Cup-a-soup with bread
- Vetkoek with mince

**Dinners:**

- Mon: Chicken fried rice
- Tue: Bobotie with yellow rice
- Wed: Beef mince & potato bake
- Thu: Portuguese chicken with peri-peri rice
- Fri: Crumbed fish & chips
- Sat: Chicken pie (puff pastry)
- Sun: Lamb neck stew with samp & beans

---

### **WEEK 4:**

**Breakfasts:**

- Rotation continues

**Lunches:**

- Vetkoek with cheese
- Chicken & salad wraps
- Pasta with butter, cheese & herbs

**Dinners:**

- Mon: Chicken livers peri-peri with pap
- Tue: Beef mince lasagna
- Wed: Chicken kebabs with couscous
- Thu: Pilchards in tomato sauce with rice
- Fri: Durban lamb curry with roti
- Sat: Boerewors rolls with fried onions
- Sun: Roast beef with Yorkshire pudding

**Then repeat Week 1!**
